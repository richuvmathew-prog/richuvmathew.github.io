⚙️ ITC Industry Regulations & Knowledge:

  • ITC Test Performance: Scoring 85 marks awards a 2-year permit duration.
  • Airport Taxi Colors: Black.
  • Identifying an ITC Inspector: Check their official credentials / permit documentation.
  • Road Speeds: Yes, roads are bound by specific posted speeds.
  • Mission of an ITC Inspector: Monitor taxi operations.
  • Plastic Fleet Separator: Features exclusively inside the Smart Taxi.
  • MDT Streamax Capabilities: Drivers can print both the old receipt and current receipt.
  • ITC Taxi Inspection Hub: Located in Baniyas.
  • Abu Dhabi Emirate Airports: Exactly 2 Airports total (Zayed International Airport and Al Ain International Airport).
  • Driving Testing Centers: Baniyas and Al Ain.
